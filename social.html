Managed Social Overview

Dealer.com’s dedicated social media strategists provide a balanced approach to social media marketing and dealership community management. Campaigns and social activity consistently align with your overall digital strategy and encompass a complete range of services to optimize your social identity and reach.

Key Features:

  • Partnership – Planning and execution of your dealership’s optimal social strategy.
  • Social Posting – Automotive retail’s leading social experts and applications to actively engage your clients with organic, unique, and targeted posts.
  • Reputation Management – Powerful listening tools to uncover a true online sentiment towards your dealership. Social strategy is created and adapted based on this rich knowledge.
  • Communication and Reporting – Monthly reports for visibility into your social performance.

Strategic Social Campaign Management

Proactive and Reactive Social Media Reputation Management

Managed Social proactively and reactively manages your social media presence across Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Instagram, and LinkedIn. Additionally, we monitor cross-platform vehicle reviews, manufacturer news, promotions, and incentives, and advertise dealer-sponsored events and community activities.
