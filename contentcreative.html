Content & Creative Overview

Dealer.com’s professional designers and copywriters are dedicated to creating compelling and strategic website content that meticulously follows your campaign strategy and goals. Partnering with your Digital Advisor, these content and creative specialists develop all campaigns according to specific dealership, group, and OEM identity.

Content and Creative benefits include:

  • A collaborative structure, backed by professional specialists for efficient campaign management.
  • Monthly campaign creation highlighting OEM incentives, dealership specials, and other sales events.
  • Automotive specialization for a complete understanding of the business of car dealerships.

Overview

Creative teams leverage unique partnerships with OEMs to build approved campaigns using the most up-to-date and manufacturer compliant media. This reduces time spent gathering assets, resulting in cost savings and creative alignment. Digital campaigns are coordinated, initiated, and managed for optimal impact.

Each campaign can include:

  • Homepage Slideshow Images
  • Campaign Landing Pages
  • Landing Page Banners
  • Creative for Specials
  • Creative for Social Visability
  • Enhanced Blog Content
  • Custom Pages
