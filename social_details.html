Managed Social

Service Details

Assigned Social Media Strategist

Managed Social comes with an assigned social media specialist that manages your social accounts, and is available to fi eld questions regarding social strategy directly

Monthly Strategy and Performance Call

Each month the social media specialist will have a scheduled call with you to discuss the performance of the previous month’s content and the strategy for the next month. Overall strategy can be aligned with other Managed Services such as SEO and Content and Creative.

Monthly Reporting

Each month we will provide a specifi c social media report that includes high-level overviews of your dealership’s performance on social channels, with a specific focus on Facebook. Using data for content performance, page engagement over time, demographics, etc., you can implement informed future strategies.

Additional Page Content Creation (pages per month)

Each month, we’ll write additional content for pages of the client’s website. This content could be for the About Us, Service, Parts, etc.,pages and will be focused on creating a consistent message showcasing what makes the dealership unique.

Strategic Monthly Posting

Social strategists implement a plan to post media at predetermined times. These posts are planned out over a month and synchronized with overall digital strategy.

Agile Positing Capability

Close contact with your strategist enables our team to post relevant content immediately.

Social Activity Across Multiple Social Channels

Proactive creation and execution of content across all social media networks.

Report

We will provide a report that looks at the performance of the campaigns and assets we’ve created for the client, including slide impressions and clicks. This report will be generated monthly and delivered in tandem with the reporting call.

Targeted Facebook Posts

In addition to general news feed posts, we can serve up content to a targeted demographic or custom audience

Real-time Reputation Monitoring to Comments and Reviews

Social media specialists provide recommended responses to both negative and positive reviews and comments.

Event Management

Upon your request, social specialists create Facebook Events on your Facebook page to promote dealership activities (BBQs, unique test drive events, etc.)— from strategy-building, con- tent writing, and asset design.

Planned Instagram Posts

Social media specialists schedule posts to your Instagram account that go live on a predetermined date and time.

Contest Management

Upon your request, social specialists will manage Facebook contests—from strategizing to content and creative asset generation to implementation.

Custom Social Creative Assets

Managed Social will create profile and cover photos as well as Facebook advertising creative you’re your request.

Advanced Social clients receive $25 of Facebook ad spend and Premium Social clients receive $75 in order to help grow their social fanbase.

Monthly Socially Relevant Blog Post

Advanced subscribers get one monthly social blog post and Premium subscribers receive two monthly blog posts specifi cally written and designed for social sharing.
